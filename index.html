Reflection

Week 1 Reflection

Week 1

My Demo teaching was assigned for the 2nd week and it is the first week of demo teaching, although our first week of demo was through online i have also learned a lot when conducting online meeting or online class. On our online teaching demo i learned that as a teacher or intrustor you must always check your students if they are listening by calling them to read or to recite, in a online set classroom you as a teacher cannot tell if your students are listening.

Week 2 Refelecton

Week 2

It was my demo teaching schedule but due to my sore throat i coudn't teach so i was move to week 5. During Week 2 of our teaching demo a learned a lot especially that you need to summarize/process your students answers, ask them probing questions to improve their critical thinking, also to manage your classroom very well, Give instructions to your student so that they accomplish the task or activity without any trouble or confusion.

Week 3 Refelecton

Week 3

For Week 3 I learned about controlling your voice, t louden our voice so that the students can hear you even at the back, creating a rubric so that the students have a guide on the score of creating their activity or task, make the motivation fun for the students to gain their attention, and because we are teaching TLE we need to improve our english speaking. I also learned that motivating your students by complementing and giving them a hand of appluase can boost their motivation and eagerness to listen and learn.

Week 4 Reflection

Week 4

I was absent during the 4th week of our demo Teaching but thank to my classmate they recorded what are the thing we should improve, that includesg giving care to your students, giving closure to the students answer and guiding them to your topic or to the right answer, Even in the power point presentations; you need to maximize and use all of the learning experience, using big font so it is easier to see and to read for the students and it is pleasing to the eyes.

Week 5 Reflection

Week 5

In my day of my demo teaching, I was nervous but I still went for it although a little rough at first i manage to keep my pace and delivered my teaching demo good. I learned that you should review your quiz if there is still time, Engage students with the topic, that you should do a review first before you dive into your motivation/ unfreezer, ask the students about their ideas and if they don't know the answer give them examples and image that is related to the topic, also to match the grade level of your student with your topic, and always be mindfull of your students safety.
